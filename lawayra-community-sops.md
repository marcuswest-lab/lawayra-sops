## 1. In-Person Meetups

Community members host local meetups in their cities for past LaWayra guests.

### Process

| Step | Action | Owner | Details |
|------|--------|-------|---------|
| 1 | **Host expresses interest** | Monica | Host reaches out via WhatsApp or Circle. Monica is the point of contact. |
| 2 | **Pre-event call** | Monica | Call with host to discuss: logistics (venue, date, time, RSVP method), share learnings from past meetups (structure helps, keep it exclusively for past LW guests), determine what promo support LW can provide. |
| 3 | **Create Circle event** | Host (Juli supports) | Host creates the event in Circle with date, time, location, and description. Juli can help if needed. |
| 4 | **Send Drip email** | Kevin | Draft email announcing the meetup. Get reviewed in WhatsApp group before sending. Send via Drip broadcast. [Drip Loom walkthrough](https://www.loom.com/share/5a22821ed2df4c828d894258dd1868e7). |
| 5 | **Social media post** | Kevin | Instagram story at minimum. Feed post if appropriate. Host can share their own content for reposting. |
| 6 | **Host Circle post** | Host | Host posts about the event in Circle to drive RSVPs and engagement. |
| 7 | **Event happens** | Host | Host runs the meetup. Take a group photo for recap content. |
| 8 | **Post-event debrief call** | Monica | Call with host: what went well, attendance, what to improve for next time. |
| 9 | **Post-event recap** | Host | Host posts recap in Circle with group photo. Kevin can repost on social media. |

### Important Notes
- Meetups should be **exclusively for past LaWayra guests** — not open to the general public.
- If multiple meetups are happening close together, combine into one Drip email to avoid email fatigue.
- When hosting at someone's home (e.g., Ashley & Matt in Denver), confirm the host is comfortable with the group size.

## 3. Sunday Community Calls

Weekly call for the LaWayra community to reconnect, share integration experiences, and support each other.

**Schedule:** Sundays, 1:00 PM – 2:00 PM Colombian time (CDT)
**Zoom:** https://us06web.zoom.us/j/82142017248
**Format:** Marcus moderates. Sam joins occasionally.

### Call Format
1. Integration reflection — where everyone is at
2. Group check-in
3. Breakout rooms for more personal sharing (small groups)
4. Come back together to discuss what support is needed
5. Closing

### Weekly Process

| Step | Action | Owner | When | Details |
|------|--------|-------|------|---------|
| 1 | **Create/duplicate Circle event** | Kevin/Juli | Monday | Recurring event may need manual duplication each week (recurring events have had issues not showing in upcoming). Include date, time in 5 time zones, Zoom link. |
| 2 | **Send Drip email invite** | Kevin | Wednesday–Thursday | Use the email template at `community-call-email.html`. Includes: date, times (CDT, PDT, EDT, BST, CEST), RSVP link, [Loom: how to RSVP](https://www.loom.com/share/0c72564773484a8397a77e08bf4a3875). |
| 3 | **Circle post reminder** | Marcus/Kevin | Thursday–Friday | Post in Circle — this also triggers an email notification to members. |
| 4 | **WhatsApp group reminders** | Juli/Kevin | Saturday | Send to past cohort WhatsApp groups. Juli has access to all groups. |
| 5 | **Instagram story reminder** | Kevin | Saturday | Post IG story the day before the call. |
| 6 | **Day-of: host call** | Marcus | Sunday 1 PM COT | Open Zoom, moderate, manage breakout rooms, ensure Fathom is recording. |
| 7 | **Post-call** | Marcus | Sunday after call | Share Fathom recording link in WhatsApp circle group. Note any follow-ups needed. |

### Notes
- First call (March 29, 2026) had 16 attendees.
- Some members in other time zones (e.g., Dubai — 2 AM) cannot attend. Future consideration: add a second call for other time zones.
- No preparation needed from attendees — "just come as you are."

- **Private Circle chat** for the cohort

## Shared Sub-Process: Sending a Drip Email

This process is used across meetups, workshops, and Sunday calls.

1. **Draft the email** — Write copy with event details, date/time (include multiple time zones), and CTA (RSVP link to Circle event).
2. **Review in WhatsApp group** — Share draft for team feedback before sending.
3. **Send test email** — Send to yourself first to check formatting and links.
4. **Broadcast via Drip** — Send to the appropriate segment/list. [Loom walkthrough](https://www.loom.com/share/5a22821ed2df4c828d894258dd1868e7).
5. **Confirm delivery** — Check Drip for open/click rates after sending.
